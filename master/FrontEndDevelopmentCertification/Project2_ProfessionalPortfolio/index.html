About Me

Chris has been an avid music educator and software developer for a number of years. His formal education began at Southern Lee High School where he served as percussion section leader. Chris then went on to Western Carolina University to obtain his Bachelor Degree in Music Education. During his time at WCU, Chris marched with the Pride of the Mountains Marching Band and studied with Mr. Bob Buckner, Mr. David Starnes, Mr. Matt Henley, Mr. Jon Henson, and Dr. Mario Gaetano.

Chris has worked with many marching bands throughout North Carolina including Franklin High School, Robbinsville High School, and Southwest Onslow High School.

Chris currently serves as the Director of Music Studies at Southern Lee High School. During Chris's time as director, the music program has grown from 16 students to 53. The band program has received numerous awards and has revitalized their Wind Ensemble.

While maintaining his respsonsibilies, Chris has devleped a passion for software development. He has completed numberous online courses, including CS50 at Harvard University, 6.001 and 6.002 at Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and "How to Code Simple Data" and "How to Code Complex Data" at the University of British Columbia. Chris has began actively searching for software development clients.

Chris currently resides in Sanford, North Carolina.
